The Wanting Compass

Answers to the most common questions. If yours isn't here, just email us — see below.

How do the two answers work?

I'm Feeling Reckless spins the compass instantly and gives you a quick dare or impulse — no questions asked. Consult It Properly asks three short questions and narrows down to one specific, direct answer based on your responses.

What does "Party mode" change?

Party mode swaps in bolder, more social-leaning answers for the same categories. If you've indicated you're of legal drinking age in your region, a small number of results may reference alcohol; otherwise those are automatically filtered out.

How do I change the language?

Tap the globe icon in the top corner of the compass screen at any time. The app currently supports English, Chinese, Japanese, Hindi, Arabic, Thai, and Russian.

Does the app save my data anywhere?

Everything stays on your device. There's no account, no login, and no server the app talks to. Deleting the app removes everything it stored. Full details are in our Privacy Policy.

Why did I get the same answer as last time?

If you've completed the full quiz before, the app remembers your last result on your device and offers a shortcut — "Same Kind of Answer As Last Time" — so you don't have to retake the quiz just to get a fresh line in that same category. Tap "Consult It Properly" instead if you want the full quiz again.

I found a bug, or have a suggestion

We'd genuinely like to hear about it — email us with as much detail as you can (what you tapped, what you expected, what happened instead) and we'll look into it.
